Nine Worlds Rough Stone
Crafting Material

The first high-energy ore in the Nine Worlds after Midgards’s birth. Its output is very scarce. The Geppetto family tried to use it to make furniture, and found that the quality of furniture influenced by the Raw Stone is excellent. It became a necessary item for the production of high-grade furniture after that.
