Skills

Alone into the Night
Lvl: 10 Passive

Even if the power he bears is detested by people, the lone hero wants to use it to repel evil. Courage and faith give him greater strength. Thanatos' Phy. DMG Inc. is increased by 30%, and his non-mounted Move Spd is increased by 30%

Burden of Sins
Lvl: 5 Passive

The person who is burdened with sins will bring curses and misfortune to anyone who comes near him. When attacked, he has a 30% chance of making the enemy enter [Poisoned] and [Fear]

Dark Circle
Lvl: 1 Buff CD: 5 sec

Restores 30 Frenzy for self, inflicts one stack of Abyssal Sear on enemies within 10m, and removes one debuff from self.

Demonic Thrill
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 50 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

Thanatos embraces the power of the magic sword and transforms into a powerful Demon King. Thanatos increases his Max HP by 30% and Pen. by 20%. He transforms into a demon and enters the Endure state for 30 seconds

Heart of Steel
Lvl: 5 Buff

The Swordsman Skill that Thanatos learned during his human days. Thanatos increases his DMG Reduc. by 90% and Abnormal Status Res by 15%. Lasts 5 seconds. Frenzy cost: 35. After using the skill, Sword Move will be reset as [Calamity]

Heavy Roll
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1 sec SP: 180 Range: 6

Chops the specified enemy and tears the void apart, dealing P.ATK*3600%*(1+Abyssal Sear count) as P.DMG to enemies within a 2*8m area along the path. This skill can cancel backswings.

Magic Sword Mastery
Lvl: 10 Passive

Having mastered the swordsmanship, Thanatos has a 50% chance to completely block the enemy damage when wielding a sword. Every point of Intellect will additionally increase 10 points of M.ATK

Nightmare
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 21 sec SP: 300 Range: 4

Thanatos unleashes the power in his magic sword, causing enemies within 4m of him to be haunted by nightmares, forcing them into [Nightmare] for 4 seconds. Enemies in [Nightmare] state can not move and attack and will return to normal after they take 2 attacks. When the state ends, all the buffs on enemies will be removed, and the cooldown time is fixed and can't be reduced

Phase 1 - Calamity
Lvl: 5 Attack Physical CD: 1 sec Range: 8

Frenzy cost: 5. Thanatos sends out a Bladesoul toward a designated direction, dealing (Atk + M.Atk) x 1800% Phy. DMG to enemies in front of him while applying a stack of Abyssal Mark. Abyssal Mark lasts 12 seconds. This skill may interrupt the back animation

Phase 2 - Heavenly Retribution
Lvl: 5 Passive

After using 【Calamity】, the skill transforms into 【Heavenly penalty】 for 5 seconds. Consumes 20 Frenzy points to leap to the designated area, dealing physical damage to enemies within a 3-meter radius upon landing, equal to (Atk + M.Atk)*2400%*2^Abyss Mark stacks. Reduces the enemies' Move Spd by 80% for 1 second, clears all area effects within the region, and applies one stack of Abyss Mark. This skill can interrupt post-action delay.

Phase 3 - Demonic Dance
Lvl: 5 Passive

After using 【Heavenly penalty】, the skill changes to 【Demonic Dance】 for 5 seconds. Consumes 25 Frenzy points to disappear on the spot for 1 second, then performs a 【Slash】 on enemies within 4 meters, dealing (Atk + M.Atk) * 2800% * 2^Abyss Mark stacks physical damage. Additionally, it puts them into a 1-second 【Nightmare】 state and applies one layer of Abyss Mark. This skill can interrupt the ending lag.

Phase 4 - Finish Move
Lvl: 5 Passive

Within 5 seconds of casting [Demonic Dance], the move is switched to [Finish Move]. Thanatos teleports behind an enemy, dealing Phy. DMG equal to (Atk +M.Atk) x 3200% x 2 ^ number of Abyssal Mark stacks to all enemies along the path. He will remove the Abyssal Marks on enemies who take damage with each stack restoring 10 points of Frenzy to himself. This skill may interrupt the back animation

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

Dark Fate
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Thanatos builds up 20 points of Frenzy every 5 seconds, with each increasing Demi-human Race DMG Reduc. by 0.12%, up to 100 points. Every use of sword move, Pen. is increased by 3% for 8 seconds, stacking up to 3 times.

Lv4. Max Frenzy increased to 150.

Lv7. [Phase 1 - Calamity]'s attack range is changed into a circle and all sword moves attaches a [Heavy Roll]
