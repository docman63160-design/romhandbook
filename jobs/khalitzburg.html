Skills

Blossom tea
Lvl: 5 Passive

Increases Khalitzburg'a debuff skill ATK by 30%. For every 1% of P.Pierce, increases Ignore P.DEF by 1%.

Echo of Petals
Lvl: 5 Attack Physical CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 3

Khalitzburg stabs at the 3*6m area in the front, dealing P.DMG equal to (P.ATK＋M.ATK)*1800% to the enemy and dropping 3 [Rose Petals] near self.

Flower of Desperation
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 150

Khalitzburg strengthens its own sword, increasing its Pen. and Skill Damage by 30%, and has a 60% chance of causing the enemy to fall into 【 Bleeding 】 when attacking. 【 Bleeding 】 effect can be stacked. The strengthening effect lasts for 120 seconds.

Guard the Spring Day
Lvl: 10 Passive

When Khalitzburg picks up its dropped 【Rose Petals】, it increases the Holy Armor level of itself and nearby 9 meters teammates 【Knight of Glory】 by one level for 10 seconds, and recovers its own VIT*50 HP per second during the duration (the effect is 4 times in PVP or GVG). The recovery effect can stack.

Guide of Rose
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases DMG of self and teammates nearby to [Bleeding] and [Cursed] enemies by 30%.

Knight of Glory
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Range: 8 Cast Time: 4

Convert the armor attribute of yourself and all teammates within 9 meters to Holy attribute, while reducing all elemental damage received by 50%, lasting for 60 seconds.

Memory of Petals
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1.5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 40 Range: 3 Cast Time: 2

Khalitzburg uses her sword to create a gale beneath herself, dealing P.DMG equal to (P.ATK＋M.ATK)*2160% to all enemies within 4m every 0.5s and reducing their MOV SPD by 60%. The gale lasts 3s. Up to 3 gales can exist at once.

Mirror Flower
Lvl: 10 Passive

In 【Memory of Petals】, it reduces the ranged physical damage and magical damage received by all teammates by 30%, and the effect on oneself is doubled.

Whisper of Rose
Lvl: 5 Attack Physical CD: 24 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec Range: 12

Khalitzburg deals P.DMG equal to (P.ATK＋M.ATK)*4200% to enemies within 12m and turns all [Doom] to [Othershore Doom], increasing HP Loss received by 30%. No debuff received can be cleared. Duration of [Othershore Doom] is extended to death of its carrier or Khalitzburg. If the enemy has no [Doom], applies [Doom]. [Othershore Doom] has all effects of [Doom], and doesn't stack with [Doom]. The cooldown is fixed.

Wish Flower
Lvl: 10 Follow Physical CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Range: 6

Khalitzburg charges at an enemy, becomes invisible for 2s and attacks the enemy 7 times, each attack dealing P.DMG equal to (P.ATK＋M.ATK)*1800%. A 30% chance to inflict [Cursed] on the enemy. Stacks up to 3 times. For every 1 point of AGI she has, deals 1% more DMG. Up to 300%. When [Wish Flower] is in effect, auto picks up [Rose Petals] nearby. The cooldown is fixed.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2.5

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

War of Rose
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. ●When Khalitzburg deals active DMG to an enemy, there's a 30%% chance for her to drop one [Rose Petal] near the enemy. Lasts 8s. Up to 3 petals can exist at once.
●When a [Rose Petal] is picked up or removed, [Doom] is inflicted on all enemies within 5m, so that they deal 12% less DMG and take 12% more DMG. Lasts 5s. The effect can't be cleared.

Lv4. When a [Rose Petal] is picked up or removed, Khalitzburg uses [Memory of Petals] once at its location.

Lv7. ●Gains the [Incarnation of Rose] skill.
●If the player dies when in the [Doom] state, they revive as [Khalitzburg]. Lasts 6s.
●Enemies in the [Doom] state deal 30% less DMG to Khalitzburg.
