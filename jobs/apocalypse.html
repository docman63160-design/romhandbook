Astrology Resonance
Lvl: 10 Passive

Each lit-up Horoscope increases your Phy. Dmg Inc. by 5%.

Astrology Resonance - Guard
Lvl: 7 Passive

Each lit-up Horoscope increases Final Dmg Reduc. by 3.5% for self; this effect will be reserved for 8 seconds after [Universe Starshard] is cast.

Crescent Kick
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1.2 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 42 Range: 2.5 Cast Time: 3.5

A Moon Skill; can only be cast during the Moon Plate. Deals Phy. DMG of ATK x 1800% to enemies within 6m directly in the front and enters the Hiding State (can not be detected) for 4 seconds.

Crescent Kick - Breaking Dawn
Lvl: 7 Passive

While the caster is in the Hiding state, the damage of [Crescent Kick] is increased by 35%.

Flame Kick
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1.2 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 42 Range: 2.5 Cast Time: 3.5

A Sun Skill; can only be cast during the Sun Plate. Kicks a target and sweeps enemy units within 3m around the target, dealing Phy. DMG of ATK x 1800% and making them [Burn].

Flame Kick - Clear Sight
Lvl: 7 Passive

After casting the [Flame Kick], enters the Hyper state and can see surrounding Hiding units within 6m for 7 second(s).

Fullmoon Kick
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 3.5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 120 Range: 2.5 Cast Time: 7

A lunar skill only usable when Moon Plate appears. Deal P.DMG equal to P.ATK*2800% to enemies within 4m. For every 3 VIT/STR/DEX, deal 1% more DMG. After use, you negate M.DMG for 4s.

Fullmoon Kick - Moon Splendor
Lvl: 5 Passive

Heals (10% x Dmg dealt by [Fullmoon Kick]) points of HP for self.

Galaxy Array
Lvl: 1 Switch

After unlocking, a star chart appears behind the character, consisting of five constellations.

Each time a kick skill is used or triggered, there is a 60%% probability of lighting up a constellation. For every constellation lit, the probability decreases by 5%%.

Cannot be activated simultaneously with Rotation of Sun and Moon.

Galaxy Array - Meteor
Lvl: 5 Passive

When launching auto attacks, there is a 15% chance to summon one star of [Universe Starshard] to fall down.

Heaven Enlightenment
Lvl: 10 Passive

When Book weapons are equipped, increases self Pen. and Ignore Def by 15%.

Meteor Illusion
Lvl: 10 Passive

The Refinement of starlight grants the character the speed as quick as a meteor. Increases Attack Spd and Move Spd by 10% for self.

Peace of Stars
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 28 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Cast Time: 4

It can only be used when all Horoscopes are lit up; grants Immunity against all debuffs to self for 12 second(s).

Peace of Stars - Reborn
Lvl: 5 Passive

If the date is a multiple of 5, [Peace of Stars] will also restore 5% of Max HP for the character per second.

Rotation of Sun and Moon
Lvl: 1 Switch

After it is unlocked and activated, there will be a Time Plate emerging from the character, which will change in the following order: Sunrise - Sunset - Moonrise - Moonset.

The Sun Plate is divided into 5 areas. In different areas, the damage of Sun Skills respectively increases by 50%%/100%%/200%%/100%%/50%%, reaching the top in the central area.

The Moon Plate is divided into 5 areas. In different areas, the damage of Moon Skills respectively increases by 50%%/100%%/200%%/100%%/50%%, reaching the top in the central area.

The Time Plate switches every 30 seconds a round.

Cannot be activated at the same time with Galaxy Array.

Sun Explosion
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 3.5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 120 Range: 2.5 Cast Time: 7

Solar System skill, can only be used during the Sun Disc period. Summon the sun to cause an Explosion on the enemy, dealing *2800% physical damage to enemies within a 4-meter range. For every 3 points increase in VIT/Str/Dex, damage increases by 1%. After using, it makes you immune to physical damage for 4 seconds.

Sun Explosion - Sun Splendor
Lvl: 6 Passive

In the central area of the Sun Plate, damage of [Sun Explosion] is increased by 30%.

Universe Starshard
Lvl: 10 Buff Physical Skill Delay: 3 sec SP: 300 Range: 7

Attaches a [Star Mark] to an enemy. The [Star Mark] summons stars per second to fall on random enemies within a radius of 5m, and the total number of the summoned stars and the seconds of duration is equal to the [Number of Horoscopes Lit Up]. Each star deals Phy. DMG of ATK x 2000%. All the Horoscopes will be extinguished after the skill is cast.

Universe Starshard - Stagnation
Lvl: 4 Passive

Each star of the [Universe Starshard] reduces Move Spd by 4% for 5 seconds; can be stacked.
