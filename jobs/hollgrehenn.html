Skills

Alloy Smelting
Lvl: 5 Function

Hollgrehenn learns to craft a variety of special alloys that are useful in combat. Skill level as well as his Luk and Dex will affect the success rate of crafting. The duration of Hollgrehenn's use of special alloys on himself is increased by 50%.

Anvil Toss
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 7 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 12

Hollgrehenn throws his anvil in the target direction. The sizzling anvil deals Phy. DMG equal to Phy. ATK x(1 + Refinement Level of off-hand/10) x3200% to enemies up ahead. It also has a 30% chance to reduce the Refinement Level of the enemy's weapon to +3 for 10 secs. The chance is increased by 1% for every 10 Luk higher than the enemy. It reduces the Final Damage of the target by 30% for 10 secs when dealing damage to monsters.

Artisan's Heart
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 16 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 120 Range: 9

Hollgrehenn uses [Hammer Swing] to harden himself and a teammate, increasing melee and ranged Physical Dmg Reduc. by 60%. At the same time, their equipment and headwear cannot be destroyed and unequiped with a Move SPD cap of 150%, lasting for 10 seconds. This effect cannot be dispelled.

Blacksmith's Wealth
Lvl: 10 Passive

The wealth Hollgrehenn has built over the years gives him the strength. When Hollgrehenn uses skills, his damage is increased by 1%% for every million Zeny he has, up to 50%, and 300% against monsters.

Blasting Furnace
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 3 Cast Time: 4

Greatly increases the temperature of the furnace and has a 60% chance to inflict [Burn] on enemies within 3m around him. He reflects 15% of the fire damage he takes, while increasing his own fire damage by 50% for 30 seconds.

Blood-boiled Refinement
Lvl: 5 Passive

Hollgrehenn's passion for refinement is on fire. Every time he applies refinement upgrade effect, his Refine ATK is increased by 30 for 30 seconds. Every time he inflicts refinement reduction effect to an enemy or attack MVP/Mini monsters, he restores 5% of his max HP.

Destructive Hammer
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1.3 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 30 Range: 3

Hollgrehenn【Swing Hammer】, dealing physical damage of *2000% to enemies within 4 meters, and at the same time, there is a 20% chance to reduce the Refine level of all Equipment and headgear of the enemy by 1, lasting for 15 seconds, the probability increases by 1% for every 10 points of Luk higher than the enemy. When dealing damage to monsters, it reduces the target's Ref. Dmg Reduc by 20%, lasting for 10 seconds.

Focused Mode
Lvl: 5 LeadSkill Physical CD: 1.5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 60 Range: 3

Hollgrehenn enters Focused Mode, performing a "Swing Hammer" every 0.5 seconds, dealing physical damage equal to 100% of "Destructive Hammer" to enemies within 4 meters, while also applying the Debuff effect of "Destructive Hammer." This attack is performed a total of 7 times. During Focused Mode, Hit is increased by 50%. If used without being in the "Ultimate Tempering" state, the Upper limit of moving speed is restricted to 150%.

High-temperature Reaction
Lvl: 5 Passive

When dealing skill DMG to an enemy, he has a 50% chance to [Stun] the enemy for 3 seconds. If the target resists [Stun], its Move SPD is reduced by 30% for 5 seconds, stacking up to 3 times.

Lucky Cart
Lvl: 10 Passive

When equipped with Cart, increases LUK by 50 and MOV SPD by 20%. For every 5 LUK, increases P.DEF by 10. For every 10 LUK, increases M.DEF by 10. For every 15 LUK, increases skill DMG by 1%.

Lucky Refine
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 45 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 150 Range: 9

Hollgrehenn does [Hammer Swing] 3 times, increasing armor and headwear Refine levels of self and teammates within 15m. For every 50 LUK, increases the levels by one. Up to Refine+18. Lasts 20s. If all armor and headwear of a target have reached Refine＋15, increases P.DMG down and M.DMG down by 10% in addition. The cooldown is fixed.

Ultimate Tempering
Lvl: 3 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

Tempers his body to the extreme in a critical situation, allowing his muscles to bulge, increasing his Final Damage Bonus by 3% for every 1 level above the Weapon or Accessory Refinement Level cap. His Final Dmg Reduc. is increased by 3% for every 1 level above the Armor Refinement Level cap. Each time Hollgrehenn adds a Refinement Level reduction effect to an enemy, he forces the enemy to Auto Attack him for 1 sec. Ultimate Tempering lasts for 10 secs. The cooldown is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

The best blacksmith on the continent
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Hollgrehenn has a 40% chance to give the refinement level of one random piece of his equipment +1 each time he uses [Hammer Swing]. It lasts for 30 seconds and can briefly break the refinement level cap.

Lv4. If the refinement level of all of his armor pieces reaches 12, his Phy. DEF and M. DEF won't be ignored.

Lv7. If the refinement level of all of his weapons and accessories reaches 17, his Refine ATK will have a % Phy. ATK bonus.
