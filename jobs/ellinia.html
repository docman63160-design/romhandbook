Skills

Dolan Trick
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 22 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Range: 5 Cast Time: 5 Fixed Cast Time: 0.2

Ellinia turns the enemy into a small veggie that can't attack, banning all their passive skills, and reducing their MOV SPD. Lasts 3s.

Element Roulette
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 20 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 400 Cast Time: 5

Ellinia can turn all of her and Pipi Dragon's attacks into Wind, Earth, Water, Fire, or Holy-type attacks, and reduce DMG of the chosen type taken by herself and Pipi Dragon by 50%. Lasts 60s.

Go, Pipi!
Lvl: 10 RideChange CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

Ellinia orders Pipi Dragon to attack nearby enemies, dealing Neutral P.DMG equal to (Pipi Dragon's P.ATK＋M.ATK)*2000%. Lasts 600s. Tapping again cancels the skill's effect. Pipi Dragon carries over Ellinia's combat attributes. When it falls in battle, it recharges and returns to battle after 10s.

Intertwined Fate
Lvl: 10 Passive

When Ellinia possesses a teammate or Pipi Dragon, she restores HP equal to (Ellinia's P.ATK＋M.ATK)*5 for self and the possessed unit every 5s (the effect quadruples during PvP and GvG), and increases shield force of self and the possessed unit by 30%.

Meow Possession
Lvl: 1 RideShoulder Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 10

Ellinia can possess a teammate or Pipi Dragon to gain part of the latter's attributes or increase Pipi Dragon's attributes. Ends after 600s or when the target dies. Tapping cancels the possession. When possession is canceled, the skill enters a 15s cooldown, which is fixed. During possession, Ellinia can only use job skills.

Meow's Prisoner
Lvl: 1 RideShoulder Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 10

Ellinia can possess a teammate or Pipi Dragon to gain part of the latter's attributes. When a possessed enemy player attacks, they damage their allies as well, but Ellinia and the possessed unit cannot attack each other. The possession ends after 10s or when the target dies. Tapping cancels the possession. When possession is canceled, the skill enters a 30s cooldown, which is fixed. During possession, Ellinia can only use [Mercury Concerto]. During possession, neither Ellinia nor the target can enter [Hiding].

Mercury Concerto
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 4 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 8

Summons star stones from the sky every 0.5s to hit 3 random enemies within 8m, dealing Earth P.DMG equal to (P.ATK＋M.ATK)*3200%. Lasts 4s. The cooldown is fixed.

Pipi's Guard
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases DMG of [Go, Pipi!] by 50% and reduces Pipi Dragon's recharge time by 4s.

Rainbow Guard
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 16 sec SP: 140 Cast Time: 3 Fixed Cast Time: 1

Ellinia provides self and the possessed with a shield that can absorb DMG equal to Max HP*100%. Lasts 10s. If Ellinia is in the [Meow Possession] state, she doesn't receive the effect, while the possessed receives double effect. The effect can't be cleared. The cooldown is fixed.

Shackle Breaking
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec Cast Time: 4 Fixed Cast Time: 0.5

Removes all the control effects and debuffs on Ellinia and the possessed unit, and be immune to control effects and debuffs for the next 1s. The skill can be used when controlled. The cooldown is fixed.

Soul Exchange
Lvl: 1 SwitchRide CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 10

During [Meow Possession], you can use it on another teammate or Pipi Dragon to target the possessed instead. The cooldown is fixed.

Starlight Flow
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases own Flee by 30% and MOV SPD by 15%. When possessing a teammate or Pipi Dragon, the latter gains this passive effect.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 5

Deals (Atk100%) Dmg to the target

Mercury Contract
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. ●Ellinia possesses an enemy or teammate player, gaining part of the latter's attributes, up to 14%.
●When Ellinia possesses Pipi Dragon, Pipi Dragon deals 14% more DMG and takes 14% less DMG.
●Gainable attributes: ignore P.DEF, P.Pierce, P.DMG bonus, skill DMG bonus.

Lv4. During possession, Ellinia is invincible.

Lv7. When using [Mercury Concerto], hits the closest enemy within 5m with an additional star stone. Invincibility lasts 3s more after Ellinia cancels possession.
