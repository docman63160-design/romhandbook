Skills

Breaking Dawn
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 60 Range: 7

Use on an enemy, rendering all their reflection, block, dodge effects, and immunity to death ineffective, lasting for 10 seconds

If the target is killed by 【Strike of fire】, it will cause an explosion damage of (remaining health of the target before death *50%) to allies within a 4-meter range of the target

When the effect is dispelled, it will refresh the Cooldown Time of this skill.

Bright mirror
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 3 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 150 Range: 7

Apply Bright Mirror on the enemy, allowing all teammates to identify the enemy's armor attributes in PVP/GVG, and reduce the enemy's crit damage reduction and all elemental damage reduction by 30%, the effect lasts for 30 seconds.

Cast blade
Lvl: 10 Passive

Heinrich, for each Equipment of a dagger, increases his own Agi by 15 points and 15% movement speed; for each Equipment of a long sword, increases his own Str by 15 points and 1 meters of attack range and casting range.

Eternal Night
Lvl: 10 Passive

If [Disaster Fire Strike] kills a player, Heinrich's Max HP is increased by (target's remaining HP before death*100%). Lasts 30s. Stackable.

When [Disaster Fire Strike] is used on a monster, it doesn't cost HP, and negate P.DMG, M.DMG, and HP Loss for Heinrich in 3s.

Fire strike
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical CD: 8 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 4

Heinrich spends his remaining HP to land a deadly attack on the enemy, dealing P.ATK*(remaining HP/50000＋2500%) as P.DMG. Uses up all [Charge] stacks, each stack increasing DMG by 10%. After casting this skill, loses 80% of current HP. If the skill kills the target, restores the amount of HP the target had before hit for Heinrich. The cooldown is fixed. [Disaster Fire Strike] ignores the target's DMG reflection, DMG negation, flee, and death negation.

Glimpse
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Cast Time: 2

After a brief chant, reduce your own movement speed by 30%% and gain 【Endure】, reducing the final damage received by 90%. For every 3 attacks received, add one layer of 【Empowerment】 to yourself. The effect lasts for 6 seconds, Cooldown Time cannot be reduced, and the effect cannot be dispelled.

Monthly
Lvl: 10 Passive

When the enemy is more than 5 meters away from Heinrich, the damage dealt to Heinrich is reduced by 80%. When there are no enemies within 5 meters of Heinrich, he recovers 20% of his maximum health every 3 seconds.

Moonlight Dance
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 2

Deal physical damage of *1500% to enemies within an 8-meter range of oneself, each layer of 【Charging】 increases damage by 10%, consumes all 【Charging】, and for each layer consumed, restores 10% of the maximum health to oneself

Apply 【Mirror】 to all enemies hit

Starlight
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases skill multipliers of [Whitestar - Wind], [Whitestar - Flower], [Whitestar - Snow], and [Whitestar - Moon] by 200%. For every 6 VIT, increases DMG of these skills by 1%.

Unyielding
Lvl: 10 Passive

Triggers the power of blue blood, reducing DMG taken from monsters and HP Loss by 30%. When Heinrich's HP is above 60%, he deals 50% more DMG.

Whispering Wind
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 7 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 30

Do a backflip for 5 meters and make yourself immune to ground magic effects and ranged physical damage for 3 seconds.

White Star
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Heinrich cannot perform auto attack. When attacking, he uses [Whitestar - Wind], [Whitestar - Flower], [Whitestar - Snow], and [Whitestar - Moon] in a loop based on his ATK SPD. Heinrich can carry off-hand weapons. For each long sword or short sword he equips, his skill has a 11% Crit Rate. When landing a critical hit, he deals Crit DMG, and ignores P.DEF.

Lv4. Each stack of [Charge] increases Crit Rate of his skills by 3% and Crit DMG by 10%.

Lv7. All skills of Heinrich enjoy an AA DMG bonus and are immune to AA DMG down.
