Blurred Whisper
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 80 Range: 6 Cast Time: 5

Confuse an enemy, swap 100% of their Basic M.ATK and Basic M.DEF. Lasts 10 seconds.

Lvl: 6 Passive

Day and Night
Lvl: 10 Ensemble CD: 60 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 200

Ensemble skill, granting teammates within a 5-meter range the effects of Sun/Moon, 【Sun】 provides 30% Dmg Reduc. and recovers 10% max health per second, 【Moon】 provides 30% Magic Reduc. and recovers 10% max magic per second. The effect alternates every 5 seconds, lasts for 60 seconds, consumes 25 SP per second. It requires playing together with a bard in the team who has learned this skill. The skill level is equal to the rounding down (sum of both skill levels/2). Must equip a whip-type Equipment weapon.

End of Sun and Moon
Lvl: 10 Ensemble CD: 80 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 300

A concord skill that inflicts one stack of [Doomsday] per sec on all enemies within 5m. Each stack of [Doomsday] reduces MOV SPD of the enemy by 10% (doesn't work on monsters). The enemy dies upon gaining the 5th stack of [Doomsday] (doesn't work on monsters). Enemies out of [End of Sun and Moon]'s range deal 100% less DMG to the performer. The performance lasts for 60s, consuming 30 SP per sec. You must perform with a Bard in the team that has learned this skill. The skill level is (total skill level of the two/2) rounded down. A whip must be equipped.
