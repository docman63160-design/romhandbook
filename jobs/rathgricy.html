Skills

Blade of Order
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 1.2 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 7 Cast Time: 2

Rathgricy attacks enemies in a 3x8m area in front of her with Razorquill, dealing (P.ATK+M.ATK)*1500% every 0.5s as P.DMG. Lasts 4s. Each attack reduced the target's P.DEF by 10% for 4s. Can be stacked 5 times.

Command of Charge
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 42 Range: 9

Grants charging effect to self and all nearby Party members, making them immune to knockback, rooting and slowing effects, increasing their Move Spd by 30% for 10s

Divine Destruction
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases the Rathgricy's max HP by 20%. Attacks reduce the target's P.Pierce and M.Pierce by 30% for 8s.

Divine Verdict
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 9

Rathgricy consumes an Angel Snow Feather and leaps towards the target location after 1s, sweeping the battlefield with her weapon and dealing (P.ATK+M.ATK)*3350% as P.DMG to enemies in a 4m radius. This attack causes knock back and prevents enemies from attacking for 1s. (Works only once on the same target every 5s.)

Divine Wing Armor
Lvl: 1 RideChange CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

When used, [Divine Wing Armor] is activated. When she takes damage greater than 20%% of her max HP, she can lose an Angel Snow Feather to avoid the excess damage. [Divine Wing Armor] can be used again to cancel the status.

Flames of Pride
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 7

Ignite a Angel Snow Feather and attach it to an enemy. The feather causes the enemy to lose 3%% of max HP when it's attacked by Rathgricy. HP lost in this way cannot be restored while the effect is active. This effect lasts 15s and cannot be cleared.

Justice Aura
Lvl: 10 Passive

When Rathgricy is wielding a shield, she has a 50% chance to become immune to the damage taken. After using [Command of Charge], other teammates have a 30% chance to become immune to the damage taken. This damage immunity effect cannot be cleared.

Retribution of Order
Lvl: 5 Passive

When [Blade of Order] deals damage to an enemy, it [Immobilized] them for 3 sec at a 50% chance. If [Blade of Order] is removed, the remover will take damage equal to [Blade of Order] x800% and be Immobilized.

Sacred Retribution
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 5 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 9 Cast Time: 3

Turn the ATK attribute of self and one teammate into Holy, increase DMG to Undead and Demon enemies by 30% and increase own P.ATK and M.ATK by 30% for 30s. You can choose whether to change the teammate's ATK attribute.

Spirit Summoning
Lvl: 1 AngelCome Physical CD: 27 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 8

Rathgricy consumes all of her Angel Snow Feathers and summons the same number of angels. Each angel charges towards nearby enemies every 3s, dealing [Feather Judgment] *150%% DMG to the target and enemies in a 3m radius around the target and inflicting [Feather Judgment] debuff. The angels exist for 15s. The cooldown cannot be reduced.

Valhalla's Glory
Lvl: 10 Passive

When she is under [Valkyrie] , 100% of the restoration effect will spread to teammates within 5 meters around her. Every time she restores HP through [Valkyrie], she gains a buff for 10 sec, boosting her Holy ATK and Abnormal Status Resist by 10%, stacking up 5 times.

Valkyrie
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 75 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

Rathgricy changes her race to Angel, increases her Skill DMG bonus and Skill DMG Down by 30% and grants [Endure] to herself. When she deals skill damage, 5% of the damage dealt will be converted to HP. This effect lasts 30s and cannot be cleared.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

Four-winged Guardian
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. For every 20m of movement, Rathgricy gains one Angel Snow Feather. Rathgricy can get up to 4 feathers. When a feather is consumed, increases MOV SPD and P.DMG bonus by 3% for 18s. Can be stacked 4 times.

Lv4. When a feather is consumed, grants immunity to P.DMG and M.DMG for 2s and removes one debuff.

Lv7. When dealing DMG, reduces the cooldown of [Valkyrie] by 1s.
