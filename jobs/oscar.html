Skills

Distorted Space
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 149 Range: 9 Cast Time: 4

A field is generated in the specified area, reducing the enemy's Move Spd by 33%% per second within the field. After accumulating for 3 seconds, enemies will enter a 【Still】 state, unable to take any actions. The 【Still】 state can last a maximum of 8 seconds and will end early if either the time limit is reached or the enemy takes damage exceeding 50% of their maximum HP. Once ended, the timer resets. The 【Distorted Space】 effect lasts for 30 seconds; the 【Still】 effect cannot be dispelled and does not affect MVP/Mini monsters. Only one 【Distorted Space】 can exist at any given time, and 【Distorted Space】 can be cast on 【Earth Field】.

Fate Retrospect
Lvl: 10 ShiftPos CD: 18 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 236 Range: 12

Designate a teammate/enemy and revert them to their position 5 seconds ago or beside Oscar. The duration of any dispellable Buff/Debuff on the target teammate/enemy will be restored to its original length, and the teammate will recover 100% health. Players cannot be removed from the current map. The reversion effect is ineffective against monsters.

Key resonance
Lvl: 1 Passive

Oscar's DMG against All Species and All Species mitigation are equivalent to the highest corresponding Species attribute values. Teammates of the Dragon Species in the team can receive the same effect.

Magnetic field effects
Lvl: 10 TimeSpaceJourney Magic CD: 2.5 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Range: 8 Cast Time: 2

Generate a Rift at the specified location, dealing non-elemental damage equal to M.Atk*2800% within a 4-meter radius, and pulling the nearest enemy within a 6-meter range to the target location；1 second later, deal another instance of non-elemental damage with the same multiplier, increasing the damage radius to 6 meters.

Oath of Time
Lvl: 5 Passive

When Oscar receives physical/magical damage, there is a 30% chance to restore 100% of the damage received as HP. After suffering a continuous HP Loss effect, there is a 20% chance to remove its subsequent effects.

Singularity of Silence
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Range: 6

Deals non-elemental damage equal to M.Atk*3000% to a single enemy and causes the unresolved 【Time Eclipse Bubble】 on the target to immediately inflict 50% damage, then clears 【Time Eclipse Bubble】; when actively using 【Silent Singularity】, it will spread the 【Time Eclipse Bubble】 on the target to all enemies within 5 meters.

Space-Time Gaze
Lvl: 5 MultiTimeSpaceJourney Magic CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 650 Range: 20

After selecting an area, begin channeling and release 【Magnetic Field Effect】 at the target area every second. Channeling can last for up to 10 seconds, and the release area can be adjusted during the channeling. Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Temporal Erosion Bubble
Lvl: 10 Passive

【Magnetic Field Effect】 will apply 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 to enemies; 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 deals M.Atk*140% damage per second for 10 seconds, 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 can stack, and within 【Twisted Space】, the damage dealt by 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 is doubled.

Time and Space Travel
Lvl: 5 ShiftTrap CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 180 Range: 12

Teleports itself to a designated location and transfers the 【Warped Space】 within the map to the destination as well.

Time-delay black hole
Lvl: 1 Buff CD: 45 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 500

Extend the effect of [Twisted Space] to the entire Map. After 3 seconds of continuous Slowed effect, all Players and normal monsters except Oscar enter the [Stagnation] state, unable to act for 5 seconds. The [Stasis] state will not recover from damage received and cannot be immunized. During its duration, Oscar's skill Cooldown Time is reduced by -100%%. After the time ends, the [Twisted Space] effect on units will be removed, and they will be immune to the [Stasis] effect for 15 seconds. When released in GVG/Field, it changes to affect units within a 12-meter range. This effect is invalid for Players in non-PVP/GVG Maps. The Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Time Wormhole
Lvl: 5 SummonSyncUser CD: 25 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 400

Summon a time barrier that follows you, hiding yourself and teammates within a range of 6 meters, and making it impossible to receive damage from enemies outside the barrier. Lasts for 12 seconds, with a fixed Cooldown Time that cannot be reduced.

Undercurrents surge
Lvl: 10 Passive

Oscar and his teammates increase the damage received by the target by 10%% for 5 seconds when using each different attribute to attack the enemy; when Oscar deals Neutral Dmg, the 0.3 element counter coefficient is increased.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic Range: 6

Deals (M.Atk100%) M.Dmg to the target

Temporal Decay
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. The damage of 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 is converted into HP Loss. Each stack of 【Time-Corroded Bubble】 reduces the trigger interval of ongoing HP Loss effects on the target by 1%. Ongoing HP Loss effects include abnormal conditions such as 【Poison】, 【Bleeding】, as well as skill effects like 【Alight】 and 【Temporal Erosion Bubble】.

Lv4. Enemies affected by 【Temporal Erosion Bubble】 will suffer the 【Singularity of Quietus】 effect every 3 seconds, and 【Temporal Erosion Bubble】 will not be cleared.

Lv7. Each time the effect of Lv.4 Trait is triggered, the remaining Cooldown Time of all Oscar’s class skills is reduced by 1 second.
