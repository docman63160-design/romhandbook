Skills

Adventurer
Lvl: 5 Passive

Bell can achieve Rapid Growth through adventuring. Every 10 adventure levels will enhance the Attr Effect of 10% of 【The Hestia Knife】, up to a maximum of 5 times.

Argonaut
Lvl: 1 LeadSkill

Guide to gain energy, when the energy progress reaches 100%%, you will acquire the 60-second [Argonaut] state: transforming skills into [Limit Off]. During guidance, the energy progress increases by +10%% per second, and using [Fire Bolt] also increases the energy progress by +10%%. The guidance state can be interrupted at any time, and the energy progress will be preserved.

When using [Limit Off], your Move Spd increases to the limit, each Auto Attack deals an additional attack, the [Rapid Escape] skill effect is raised to 100%%, lasting for 10 seconds. After the duration ends, the [Argonaut] state is lifted. [Limit Off] has a fixed Cooldown Time of 30 seconds which cannot be reduced.

Blade Resonance
Lvl: 10 Passive

Bell's magic Critical effect receives an additional bonus. Every 2%% of Crit.Dmg will increase the magic Critical damage by 1%. When Auto Attack triggers a Critical, it deals M.Atk * 100% magic damage to all enemies within a 3-meter radius of the target.

Fire Bolt
Lvl: 10 RocketLightning Magic CD: 4 sec SP: 300 Range: 7

Gain 1 charge every 5 Auto Attacks, up to a maximum of 5 charges. Deals fire attribute magic damage equal to (Atk + M.Atk) * *1000% to the target and enemies within 5 meters. This does not consume an attack action. Upon use, all charges are consumed, and for each charge consumed, the skill is triggered an additional time.

Liaris Freese
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 450

After use, each attack increases your Critical by 1 point, Crit.Dmg by 1%%, Attack Spd by 1%%, and the attack speed cap, stacking up to 100 layers for 30 seconds. When in the same team as Ais, the stacking Speed up is doubled. Upon reaching the maximum stack, your damage dealt increases by 100%. 【Liaris Freese】 lasts for 300 seconds, and the effect cannot be removed by Clearance.

Lightning Speed
Lvl: 10 RecordPosition CD: 9 sec SP: 100 Range: 10

Instantly teleport to the enemy's side and inflict a 1-second [Taunt] effect on the target. When used on Monsters, the duration of the [Taunt] effect is extended to 5 seconds. Using the skill again within 5 seconds allows you to return to the original position.

Rapid Escape
Lvl: 10 Passive

Bell has a 30% chance to perfectly Flee from damage and Debuffs when attacked or subjected to negative effects. Auto Attacks can increase the chance of this Flee effect by 1%, lasting for 2 seconds, stacking up to 30 times.

Symphony of Wind and Fire
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 400 Range: 7 Cast Time: 3

Bell and Ais join forces, merging the powers of Wind and Flame to unleash a magnificent and destructive attack. Ais summons a strong Wind element to gather enemies within a 7-meter radius, while Bell simultaneously releases a powerful Flame magic. The wind fuels the flames, forming a fiery storm that deals M.Atk*2400% Fire attribute magic damage and M.Atk*2400% Wind attribute magic damage per second to all enemies within the area for 5 seconds. If Ais is not in the team, only the Flame damage effect will be applied.

The Guardian of the Flame Wind
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 90 Range: 9

Bell and Ais jointly cast a defensive skill, using the power of wind to Protect Party and enhance their speed. Ais increases the Move Spd of herself and all Teammates within a 9-meter radius by 10% and their Flee by 50. Meanwhile, Bell provides a damage Absorb Shield to Teammates within the area, with the Absorb value equal to his max HP * 50%, lasting for 30 seconds. If Ais is not in the team, only the damage Absorb Shield will be provided. The Shields and buffs provided by Bell and Ais can stack.

The Heroic Shot
Lvl: 1 Attack CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 3

Unlocked for use in the 【Limit Off】 state, deals an ultimate strike to a single target for (Atk + M.Atk) * 3200%. 【The Heroic Shot】 is neither "magic" nor Slash, ignoring enemy Reflected damage, magic immunity, Magic Reduc., and M.Def. When calculating damage bonuses, it is treated as magic damage and can trigger a Critical.

Unyielding Will
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 150

Develop new effects through accumulated abilities. For every attribute reaching 300, gain the following enhancement effects in sequence, lasting 300 seconds:
【Luk】: Spell Crit.Dmg increases by 50%
【CC effect Res】: CC Effect Res increases by 50%
【Escape】: Move Spd increases by 30%
【Combo】: The charge limit of 【Fire Bolt】 increases by 5

Yearning or longing
Lvl: 10 Passive

Bell takes 50% reduced damage from females and monsters, except for Ais. When fighting against females or monsters, Bell gains 50 All attributes for 10 seconds.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2.5

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

The Hestia Knife
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Bell's main hand is always equipped with The Hestia Knife, a weapon personally forged by Hephaistos, the God of the Forge, at Hestia's request, costing two hundred million valis. It boasts a high magic conductivity rate. The weapon itself possesses ability values and can undergo Growth based on the wielder's abilities, making it a living weapon. Its stats change according to the following values:
● Str: Every 10 points increase by *0.01%* lifesteal.
● Agi: Every point increases *0.01%* Auto Attack damage.
● VIT: Every 10 points restore *0.01%* of max HP per second.
● Int: Every point increases *0.01%* Ignore M.Def.
● Dex: Every point increases *0.01%* Phy. Dmg Inc. and Pen.
● Luk: Every point increases *0.01%* magic Critical chance.

When performing an Auto Attack, it deals additional magic damage equal to 100% M.Atk and can trigger magic Critical.

Lv4. You can select two attributes of The Hestia Knife to double their effects.

Lv7. During Auto Attack, The Hestia Knife executes smooth Successive attacks, eliminating the pre- and post-attack animation delays, fully matching the attack frequency with Attack Spd.
