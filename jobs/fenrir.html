Skills

Barbarity
Lvl: 5 Passive

Each time you shoot a Penetrating arrow, reduce the Cooldown Time of 【Moon Shackle】 by 1 second (effective up to once per second). When using 【Moon Shadow Barb】, restore 25% of your own maximum health and magic.

Bloodmoon
Lvl: 10 Passive

For every 1%% of health the enemy loses, Fenrir's damage to them increases by 2%. When killing an enemy, it will recover its own health (remaining health before the target's death *50%), with a maximum effect of once per second.

Frost Moon Arrow
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 3 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300

Let the cold moonlight condense into arrows, converting the Offensive Attribute into a water attribute, increasing self 30%'s Auto Attack Dmg Inc. for 120 seconds. At the same time, Fenrir's Auto Attack will reduce the enemy 30%'s attack speed and movement speed for 5 seconds, stackable up to 3 times.

Ice Jail
Lvl: 10 FenrirUltimate CD: 35 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 8

●Tow up to 4 enemies within 8m to self and apply magic chains.

●Enemies with magic chains can't speed up, use reposition skills, or enter Hiding.

●Enemies with magic chains take 20% more DMG. When Fenrir performs AA to bound enemies, he shoots other bound enemies once as well.

Magic chains last 8s. The cooldown is fixed.

Lunar eclipse
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases Fenrir's Water DMG and Max SP by 50%. When you're dying, turns all the remaining SP into your HP, and prevents HP loss for 1s. Conversion rate is remaining SP*your LUK*30% (the effect quadruples during PvP and GvG). Triggered only once every 6s.

Moon Shackle
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 100 Range: 7 Cast Time: 2

Bind an enemy with a curse chain made of moonlight, making them unable to move, reducing their 80% physical and magical attack for 5 seconds. If this effect is dispelled, transfer the 【Moon Shackle】 effect to the target who applied the dispel.

Moon Shadow Barb
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 4 sec SP: 50

Roll 5 meters in the direction of the joystick with the help of the shadow under the moonlight, and shoot 3 Penetrating arrow arrows in a fan shape at the nearest enemy, making yourself invulnerable to losing health within 1 second after rolling. This skill can be used twice in a row, and the Cooldown Time cannot be reduced.

Moon Wolf Assault
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 7 Cast Time: 4

Fenrir orders Moon Wolf to attack an enemy, dealing P.DMG equal to P.ATK*1000% per sec, and banning them from entering [Hiding]. Lasts 8s. Each bite has a 30% chance to inflict [bleeding]. Moon Wolf can land critical attacks. The Crit Rate is (own Crit－target's critical guard)/2. Critical attacks ignore P.DEF.

Moon Wolf's Rage
Lvl: 10 Passive

The damage of 【Moon Wolf Raid】 is increased by 30%, and there is a 30% chance that a 【Moon Shackle】 will be added to the enemy when 【Moon Wolf Raid】 critically hits.

Twilight Attack
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 150

The prophecy said that Fenrir would bring doom to the gods. It increases Fenrir's P.Pierce by 30% and Crit DMG by 50% for 30s. The 5 following AA trigger Piercing Arrow.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 CountExtra Physical Range: 7

Deals (Atk100%) Dmg to the target

Moon Shot
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Fenrir performs a normal attack 4 times, and the next normal attack will shoot a Penetrating arrow that penetrates all enemies, causing (normal attack *300%) damage to the enemies on the path

Lv4. The number of attacks required for Penetrating arrow is -1

Lv7. Penetrating arrow deals double damage to monsters and inflicts 10% current health loss on the target player.
