Skills

Advanced Composition
Lvl: 1 Passive

After learning it, You could get different composition for selfie. You will like it!

Close-up Shot
Lvl: 1 Passive

Expands the zoom range to 200mm. Time to get more details~

First Aid
Lvl: 2 Heal CD: 10 sec Range: 1

Quickly restores (100 + BaseLv*5) points of HP

Free View
Lvl: 1 Passive

After learning it, You could unlock free view provided by the top right button in the camera to take better photos!

Might Field
Lvl: 1 Passive

Increases your STR for higher load Could carry more items. Each 5 Str expand the bag by 1 slot

Monster Targeting
Lvl: 1 Passive

Press and Hold the auto-fight button to load felt monsters within 20m (Marked on the mini map). Select one to search and fight.

Play Dead
Lvl: 2 FakeDead CD: 10 sec Range: 1

Instantly enters Play Dead status, so the attacker loses the target and leaves the battle. Every 1 sec, restores HP equal to 10% Max HP and SP equal to 10% Max SP for self.

Poring Selfie Stick
Lvl: 1 Passive

Now the camera could see further and wider. Try Poring Selfie Stick in the selfie mode!

Preparation Skill
Lvl: 1 Passive

By tapping any skill right after you release one skill, this skill can be automatically cast after Cast Delay/cooldown.

Protect Party
Lvl: 1 Passive

Select it so when any party mate is under monster attack, others in auto-fight will go help him/her out. If multiple party mates are under attack, or if you are also under attack yourself, it chooses to protect the nearest one.

Rapid Riding
Lvl: 1 Function

After you learn the skill and enable auto ride, the mount in your Adventure Backpack will be auto-equipped when you tap any location on the minimap or world map.

Rest on Site
Lvl: 1 Passive

When the user sits, every 5 sec restores 10% max HP and SP

Wide Angle
Lvl: 1 Passive

Expands the zoom range to 24mm. Good for Landscape Photographer!

Nebula Summon
Lvl: 1 GoMap CD: 10 sec

Summons a Cat-Ear Nebula that allows fast transportation to Lasagna.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 1.5

Deals (Atk100%) Dmg to the target

Thump
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical CD: 10 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 1 Range: 2

Deals (ATK 200%) points of Phy. DMG to surrounding enemies within 2m, knocking them back and stunning them. Gains the Endure effect for 1 sec at the same time.
