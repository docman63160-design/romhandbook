Skills

Dragon Breath Permeate
Lvl: 10 Passive

[Streamer of Fallen Night] will reduce enemies' Phy. ATK, M.ATK and M.Def by 10% for 5 seconds. The effect can stack up to 5 times and cannot be dispelled.

Flower's Protection
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 150 Range: 7 Cast Time: 5

With the power of Yggdrasil, Nidhogg distorts the space within a 2-meter radius around herself and [Draconic Tribesmen]. Upon entering the space, Nidhogg and her teammates take 60% less damage from enemies outside. The barrier lasts 8 seconds. This skill can be cast within [Earth Field] and [Fire Rain].

Force of Life
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 3 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 150 Range: 9

Unleash power of nature to heal self and [Dragon's Subjects], restoring HP equal to (own M.ATK*2＋own INT*150), and restore the same amount in the next 3s (the effect quadruples during PvP and GvG).

Light of Divine Judgement
Lvl: 5 Passive

When Nidhogg attacks, she has a 40% chance to purify herself and [Draconic Tribesmen], removing one debuff.

Magical Dragon Scale
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases DMG of [Streamer of Fallen Night] by Nidhogg's current SP/Max SP*100%; increases Dark DMG of Nidhogg and [Dragon's Subject] by 30%.

Queen of Dragon
Lvl: 10 Buff Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 9

She views one of her teammates as a [Draconic Tribesman], using the Power of Blood Resonance to increase all attributes of herself and the [Draconic Tribesman] by 30 points for 300 seconds. If the [Draconic Tribesman] is more than 15 meters away from Nidhogg, the effect is temporarily disabled. Can't view other Nidhogg as [Draconic Tribesman]

Redemption of Fallen Night
Lvl: 5 Passive

When Nidhogg dies, she unleashes massive life energy and restores 100% HP and MP of all teammates while reviving dead teammates within a 5-meter range and restoring their full HP and MP. This effect can trigger up to once every 8 seconds

Savior's Flower
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 18 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 300 Range: 9 Cast Time: 5 Fixed Cast Time: 0.5

Nidhogg unleashes the massive life energy of Yggdrasil to prevent herself and [Draconic Tribesmen] from dying while reviving dead teammates within a 5-meter range, restoring all of their HP and MP for 6 seconds. The Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Streamer of Fallen Night
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 180 Range: 7 Cast Time: 8

Nidhogg attacks one enemy, dealing M.ATK*4200% as Dark M.DMG to the target and enemies within 3m. A 20% chance to reduce final healing of all enemies by 50% for 5s. When Streamer of Fallen Night deals DMG, there's a 25% chance to turn the target's armor type to Holy for 5s. You can choose to disable this effect.

Twilight of the Gods
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 7

Temporarily view all teammates on the map as [Dragon's Subjects], and change own race to Dragon for 8s. The cooldown is fixed. Cannot view other Nidhoggs as [Dragon's Subject].

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic Range: 7

Deals (M.Atk100%) M.Dmg to the target

Fetters
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Reduces damage taken by Nidhogg and [Dragon Tribe] by 5%

Lv4. Nidhogg's attacks convert 20% of the damage dealt into Shields for himself and [Dragon Tribe], lasting for 5 seconds

Lv7. When Nidhogg deals Skill Damage or [Dragon Tribe] attacks deal damage, Nidhogg inflicts an additional instance of [Streamer of Fallen Night] damage on the target, up to 5 times per second.
