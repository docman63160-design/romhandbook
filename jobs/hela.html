Skills

Black Party
Lvl: 1 Buff CD: 60 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

Unlocks when you've converted over 40 stat pts. Hela declares the start of party, enhancing all active skills:
★[Garm] summons one extra Garm illusion.
★Hellhound attacks enemies within [No Near the Dog]'s range once per sec.
★[Bottled Ghost] applies 2 stacks of [Depression] each time.
★[Fatal Dream] always deals max Execute DMG to enemies.
★[Bloodline of the Deceased] works on nearby teammates. Duration for teammates halves.
★[Forgetful Wine]'s forgetfulness duration＋4s
★[Deadly Poison] applies 3 stacks of poisoned and Poison DMG.

The party lasts 40s. The cooldown is fixed.

Bloodline of the Deceased
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Cast Time: 4 Fixed Cast Time: 0.5

Hela has the Bloodline of the Deceased and can turn own HP loss effect to healing at a rate of 100% for 18 seconds, and the effect can not be cleared

Bottled Ghost
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 6 Cast Time: 4

Throw a distressed Phantom bottle at a distant enemy, dealing poison attribute magic damage equal to M.Atk *3200% to the target and enemies within a 2.5-meter radius, and inflicting 【Distress】. Over the next 15 seconds, 【Distress】 deals 10% damage per second. The 【Distress】 effect can stack up to 15 layers. The 【Distress】 inflicted on monsters cannot be subject to Clearance, and when the 【Distress】 stacks exceed 10 layers, the target monster's Armor attribute will no longer resist poison attributes.

Castigate
Lvl: 10 Passive

When the target has 0 Quality, for every 5 LUK Hela has, she deals an extra 1% skill DMG to the target. For every 5 INT/DEX/LUK Hela has, deal 2% skill DMG to monsters.

Deadly Poison
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 8 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 80

Drink Fatal Poison, non-poison element damage you take is reduced by 50%, poison element attack increased by 20%, for 30 seconds. If in the [Bloodline of the Deceased] state, also put yourself into [Poisoned] state

Demon Turntable
Lvl: 5 Passive

When you are in an abnormal state, you have a 25% chance to infect all your abnormal states to the enemy when you deal damage or take damage

Fatal Dream
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 8 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 440 Range: 6 Cast Time: 6

Hela locks onto an enemy causing it to fall into a sweet nightmare, and Nightmare Scatter deals M. ATK *4500% Poison M. DMG to it and enemies 5 meters around it. For every 10 attribute points reduced from the opponent by interference, an additional 4% of damage is added. If the opponent's remaining HP is less than 10%, they will be executed (each level of [Frustration] increases the execute limit by 3% up to 15%, execute is not effective against monsters)

Forgetful Wine
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 300 Range: 7 Cast Time: 8

Born in the Niflheim epidemic, the forgotten will truly perish, Hela spreads the disease to the enemy, the target will forget the skill for 4 seconds after using it, and the epidemic lasts for 10 seconds. The cooldown time is fixed and cannot be reduced

Garm
Lvl: 10 SummonDog CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 300

Summons Garm the hellhound from the void. It follows Hela and deals Hela's M.ATK*1600% as Poison M.ATK every time. Hellhounds stick around. Whenever a hellhound attacks, increases HP Loss of the target by 1% for 5s. Stacks up to 30 times. When Hela receives fatal damage, removes one hellhound, negates damage for 1s and recovers 30% HP.

Hide and Seek
Lvl: 5 Passive

When Hela uses skills to add effect for herself or her teammates, it will give her [Hiding], and improve Skill DMG Reduc. by 15% for 8 second(s)

No Near the Dog
Lvl: 5 SendDog CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 6

Order Hellhound to roar, all enemies within 5 meters will have all of their attributes reduced by 10 point(s), and enemies who die within this range cannot revive on the spot, effect lowering and the roar lasting 10 seconds

Rabies Vaccination
Lvl: 10 Passive

Increases Hellhounds' damage by 30% and redirects 5% damage to Hela's HP

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Physical Range: 2

Deals (Self Atk*100%) damage to an enemy target

Thump
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic CD: 10 sec SP: 1 Range: 2

Deals (M.ATK 200%) points of M. DMG to surrounding enemies within 2m, knocking them back and stunning them. Gains the Endure effect for 1 sec at the same time.

Phantom Interference
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Each time an attack is made on a player, the highest value of the target's attribute points are reduced by 10 points for 30 seconds
Each time an attack is made on a monster, its INT, DEX, and LUK are increased by 1 points for 30 seconds
(the above effects take effect at most once in 3 seconds)

Lv4. 's attributes reduced by an attack on a player are converted to its attributes at a rate of 30%

Lv7. . Hela can freely choose the attributes to interfere with through the Adventure Skills and double the duration of the effect
