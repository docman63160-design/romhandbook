Skills

Abyssal Spear
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 81 Range: 7 Cast Time: 4.5

Turn rocks within the range into hard spears that burst out of the ground beneath the target, dealing M.ATK*2000% Earth M.DMG to enemies within 3m, removing invisibility. A 100% chance to inflict bleed. When attacking Lv.1 Earth armor, ignore the elemental counter.

Disaster Panic
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 8 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100

Jörmungandr brings endless disasters, allowing his skills to deal Crit DMG, dealing 200% Crit DMG. Lasts 8s or for 10 skill attacks. Jörmungandr restores 5% of max HP every sec within the duration.

Fire Breath
Lvl: 1 Passive

Auto attack is replaced by lava spraying, which deals M.ATK*1000%% Fire M.DMG to the target and enemies 2m or less from them. For every 1 [Serpent Echo], sprays at one more enemy. When there's only 1 enemy, each [Serpent Echo] increases damage by 50%%. [Fire Breath] receives [Disaster Panic] and [Snake Poison] effects.

Invasion
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 3 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 192 Range: 6 Cast Time: 6

Send a rapidly advancing sandstorm at the specified direction, dealing M.ATK*2400% Earth M.DMG to enemies in the 3*10m area in front of it. When attacking Lv.1 Earth armor, ignore the elemental counter.

Mortal World Python
Lvl: 1 Buff CD: 45 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec

●Jörmungandr turns into a great serpent, restores HP to full, increasing his max HP by 100%% and Humanoid DMG down by 50%%, and inflicts [Fear] on nearby enemies (cannot be negated or resisted) for 1s.
●When in the serpent form, unlocks [Fire Breath] and [Venom Erosion]
●The serpent turns AoE effects of enemies on its path into [Serpent Domain] that lasts 4s.
●The serpent transformation lasts 15s. Its cooldown is fixed and can't be reduced.

Ouroboros
Lvl: 10 Passive

Each [Serpent Echo] increases Jörmungandr's Earth DMG, Fire DMG, Ignore M.DEF, and DMG to monsters by 10%, and duration of [Serpent Echo] increases by 30s.

Plunder of Fate
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 7 Cast Time: 5

Use it on an enemy to transfer the clear-able buffs they gain within 7s to self and steal 30% of their M.Pierce. The stollen Pierce cannot stack.

Serpent Domain
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 6

●Creates a Serpent Domain at the specified location. When Jörmungandr is in the domain, ATK of all his debuff skills increases by 30%. When an enemy is in the domain, all their debuff res is reduced by 50%. The Serpent Domain lasts 30s. There can only be one at a time.
●The domain uses [Venom Erosion] on summoned enemy units that are alive.
●Whenever [Stack-up] is triggered 3 times in the range, gains 1 [Serpent Echo]: Final DMG +10%. [Serpent Echo] lasts 10s. Up to 3 can be gained.

Singularity Warp
Lvl: 5 SpaceLeap CD: 8 sec SP: 300 Range: 8

Jörmungandr can teleport to specified locations up to 8m away via wormholes, with a 75% chance to pull enemies within 3m into [Blackholes] for 1s.

Snake Poison
Lvl: 10 Passive

When Jörmungandr deals Skill Damage, it additionally inflicts poison attribute magic damage equal to its M.Atk *1000%. This damage is doubled against monsters and reduces the target's Move Spd by 20% and the Upper limit of moving speed by 30% for 5 seconds. This effect can stack up to 3 times.

Space-time Prison
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 4 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 150 Range: 7 Cast Time: 2

Jörmungandr freezes the space and time an enemy is in, increasing their cooldown by 20%% and reducing their ATK SPD by 20%% for 8s. Up to 3 stacks. Upon gaining the 3rd stack, bans the target from using skills and auto attack. The 3rd stack doesn't work on Mini or MVP.

Time and space barrier
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 21 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 150 Cast Time: 3

Jörmungandr creates a 4m-wide space-time barrier. When in it, himself and allies gain 20% final DMG Down. Lasts 10s.

Travel Back
Lvl: 10 Passive

Whenever Jörmungandr takes damage, a 25% chance to travel back in time and restore the damage taken*100% as HP.

Venom Erosion
Lvl: 1 Buff CD: 7 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 4

The great serpent bites an enemy nearby, inflicting [Petrify] (cannot be negated), which lasts 5s and isn't removed when attacked. The effect can't be cleared. It doesn't work on Mini or MVP.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic Range: 6

Deals (M.Atk100%) M.Dmg to the target

Space-time Fold
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Jörmungandr can be equipped with 2 offensive or buff/debuff skills. When in [Stack-up] and auto attacking, has a 12% chance to trigger the offensive skills equipped, and a 3% chance to trigger the buff/debuff skills equipped.

Lv4. For every [Stack-up] set, attack and cast ranges increase by 1m and

Lv7. [Stack-up] number +1; when in the [World Serpent] form, triggering rate +10%.
