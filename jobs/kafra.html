Skills

Automatic Renewal
Lvl: 10 Passive

The duration of buffs added by Kafra and Debuff (including abnormal status) is extended 30%; if these effects are dispelled, there is a 30% chance for them to be re-applied.

Delivery Exception
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 137 Range: 6

Summon a mini warehouse to attack the target enemy. The warehouse can bite the enemy, rendering them unable to act. It deals M.Atk*1920% magic damage every second for 5 seconds and has a 30% probability to remove one piece of the target's Equipment, prioritizing the armor slot. Lasts for 10 seconds.

Kafra Premium Privileges
Lvl: 5 Passive

After using a single-target buff skill, the targeted teammate will be marked as a Black Card VIP, and for 25 seconds, the buff effects and health recovery effects provided by Kafra will be increased by 50%.

Love's Magic Spin
Lvl: 10 Passive

When 【Rotating Stage】 is activated, Kafra gains 30% Skill Dmg Inc., Skill Dmg Reduc., and Auto Attack Reduction. For each teammate within the stage, an additional 3% Skill Damage is increased. Other teammates inside the Rotating Stage gain (KafraAtk*2%+600) Atk and (KafraM.Atk*2%+300) M.Atk, which do not apply to other Kafra.

Overloaded Support
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 45 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 500

Must be used under the 【Rotating Stage】 state; Kafra enters overload mode, increasing Move Spd for Kafra and teammates within the 【Rotating Stage】, and expanding the radius of the 【Rotating Stage】 by 3 meters. Additionally, the attack frequency of various maces is increased by 50% for 15 seconds; if Kafra enters a near-death state during the 【Overload Support】 period, their HP will be locked at 1, and they will die after the 【Overload Support 】 effect ends. If the 【Rotating Stage】 is turned off during the 【Overload Support】 period, the 【Overload Support】 effect will end immediately. Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Professional alignment
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 145 Range: 9

Kafra enchants the selected element on the weapons of itself and one teammate, switches the Offensive Attribute to the corresponding element, and increases 20% corresponding elemental attack for 8 seconds.

Rotating stage
Lvl: 10 RideChange Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 280

Kafra pulls out its various Tooth of Warg from its pocket and automatically attacks. 【Rotating Stage】 lasts for 300 seconds, with an initial radius of 4 meters, and can be manually ended.

● Throws small Tooth of Warg at random enemies within range every second, dealing M.Atk*2400% magic damage. Each 5 points of Str increases damage by 1%.

● Throws a Medical mace every 3 seconds, healing a Teammate for (200%*M.Atk+Str*VIT/2) HP (effect is quadrupled during PVP or GVG), and grants them 15% Final Dmg Reduc. for 3 seconds. Prioritizes the Teammate with the lowest HP.

● Every 5 seconds, Kafra swings its Tooth of Warg significantly, dealing magic damage to all enemies within the roulette's range. Damage is equal to 300% of the small Tooth of Warg's damage, while healing allies within the range for 100% of the Medical mace's healing amount.

Special Discipline Mode
Lvl: 10 Passive

Kafra's mace club deals an additional 50% damage to monsters, can ignore the target's 20% racial reduction, attribute reduction, and Magic Reduc., and has a 30% chance to inflict immobilization, stun, darkness, or bleeding on enemies for 5 seconds.

Storage Service
Lvl: 1 SummonWareHouse CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Range: 8

Set up a Kafra Warehouse near the Kafra. The warehouse cannot be attacked, and Kafra teammates can click on the warehouse within a range of 3 meters. They will be unable to act and remain invulnerable for 5 seconds; during this time, they may choose to leave the warehouse. Teammates located within the rotating stage will immediately revive at the warehouse upon death. If the Kafra is more than 15 meters away from the warehouse, the warehouse will become inactive. The warehouse lasts for 30 seconds. After actively using the warehouse or triggering the revival effect, it is impossible to enter any Kafra Warehouse or revive at the warehouse for 15 seconds. Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Take a small taste~
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 18 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 155 Range: 6

Kafra presents adventurers with a special magical Food; when used on allies, it randomly grants the target 6 layers of buff effects lasting 15 seconds. When used on enemies, it steals 5 dispellable buff effects from the enemy. The buff effects of the special Food are based on various 5-star Food effects.

Teleport Service
Lvl: 5 TransferServe CD: 16 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 160 Range: 12

After chanting for 1.5 seconds, teleport yourself and teammates within the range of the [Rotating Stage] to the target point. If the warehouse is within the range, it can be teleported along as well. Must be used while in the [Rotating Stage] state.

Whoosh—Kafra takes action!
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 215 Range: 9

Summon a Kafra attendant to surround the targeted teammate, restoring 10% health and 5% SP per second, while removing one debuff effect. Lasts for 10 seconds.

Storm Gust
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic Range: 1.5

Deals (M.Atk100%) M.Dmg to the target

Heartbeat Support · Activate!
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Kafra joins the battle alongside adventurers with her Rotating Stage. When activating [Rotating Stage], Kafra's Move Spd is fixed at 100%, and the radius of [Rotating Stage]'s effect increases by 0.4 meters.

Lv4. All skill Cooldown Time -15%. When [Rotating Stage] is activated, the attack interval of various Tooth of Wargs can be reduced through skill Cooldown reduction, up to a maximum of 60%.

Lv7. Upon activating [Rotating Stage], every 15 seconds, a [Kafra Package] will spawn within a 5-meter radius and last for 25 seconds. Looting the package enhances the effect of [Love's Magic Spin] by 100% for 10 seconds and makes Kafra's next single-target skill affect all targets within the Stage. The package's effect will immediately expire when [Rotating Stage] is deactivated.
