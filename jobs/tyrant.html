Skills

Absolute Penetration
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 3 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 7

Aims at an enemy, charges briefly, and deals neutral Phy. DMG equal to ([Crack Shot] x130% +Phy. ATK x opponent's Phy. DEF/500) to the target when the charge is completed. The damage is increased by 50% for each additional second of charge. Move or click again to stop the charge and shoot directly. It can be charged for up to 5 sec and ignores Phy. DEF when fully charged. Consumes 1 bullet and requires a Rifle Gun to use. The skill ignores the effect of [Light Shield], [Auto Guard], [Sword Parry], and [Kyrie Eleison].

Bounty
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 10

Place a bounty on an enemy, so that any teammates who kill the target (including yourself) regain 100% Max HP and Max Magic. If the killer is a Gunslinger, their ammo is also fully refilled. Bounty lasts 20s and cannot be dispelled. Spends 1000 Zeny when used.

Bounty - Hunt
Lvl: 5 Passive

Reduce the DMG Reduc. And M. DMG Reduc. of targets with Bounty by 15%.

Carnage Capoeira
Lvl: 10 LeadSkill Physical CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 5

Channeling skill, continuously firing at enemies within 5 meters, dealing physical damage equal to 【Desperado】*80% every 0.3 seconds. Deals double damage to targets affected by 【Bleeding】. The channeling lasts for 3 seconds, consuming a total of 4 bullets. Must be used with Equipment: Pistol.

Carnage Capoeira - Afterimage
Lvl: 5 Passive

Take 25% reduced DMG when using Carnage Capoeira.

Carnage Capoeira - Spear Dance
Lvl: 7 Passive

Carnage Capoeira reduces the Move SPD of surrounding enemies by 70%.

Chain action
Lvl: 10 Passive

When using the Pistol skill, there is a 30% chance to deal 2 times the damage, but it will consume an additional bullet when triggered (up to 1 bullet per second).

Eagle Eye
Lvl: 5 RideChange Skill Delay: 1 sec

Open your sight, giving you a wide field of view. Rifle Gun Auto Attack and Skill range is also increased by 4m, but you cannot move. Lasts 120s. Use again to cancel the effect.

Execution
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 24 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 180 Range: 6

Execute an enemy, removing 20% of their HP and inflicting [bleeding]. Costs 1 bullet. When used on MVP or Mini monsters, inflict a fixed amount of HP Loss instead. The casting range increases along with AA range.

Fire Rain
Lvl: 10 ClearEffect Physical CD: 8 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec Range: 6

Shoot quickly, inflicting Phy. ATK*1800% in Fire Phy. DMG four times to enemies in the specified 4m area in 2 seconds. Also removes ground-based magic effects within the area. Expends 4 bullets.

Forced Dispel
Lvl: 10 Attack Physical CD: 12 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec Range: 7

Target an enemy and charge up before dealing Phy. ATK*2500% in phy. DMG to the target, dispel all buffs from the target, and prevent them from gaining buffs for 8s. Spend 1 bullet. Must be used with a rifle gun equipped. This skill ignores the effects of skills such as Light Screen, Auto Defense, Sword Block, and Kyrie Eleison's Array.

Highlord's Wrath - Despise
Lvl: 6 Passive

Gain 18% Phy. DEF Ignore when in The Rage of Tyrant state.

Mobile Ranged
Lvl: 5 Passive

You can move while using Crossfire or Carnage Capoeira, but Move SPD is reduced by 10%.

Platinum Era
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec

Expend all bullets to build a defensive membrane around yourself which blocks an attack every two shots. Lasts 60s.

Platinum Era - Defense
Lvl: 3 Passive

Platinum Era can block 3 more instances of DMG. When a Rifle Gun is equipped, it can block a 3 additional instances of DMG.

Scarlet Curtain
Lvl: 10 Passive

While using gun skills, there's a chance of causing the enemy to bleed (20% chance for pistols and 40% chance for rifle guns) for 10s. The bleeding target can't see the attacker in its sight

Scarlet Curtain - Blood Drinking
Lvl: 5 Passive

Scarlet Curtain's bleeding effect +0.5% HP/second

Shower of Fire - Burn
Lvl: 4 Passive

The Rage of Tyrant
Lvl: 10 Buff CD: 30 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 40

Increase your own 30%'s Pen. and skill damage for 15 seconds. Killing a target during the effect duration can reset the duration.

Thorough Through - Head Shot
Lvl: 7 Passive

When aiming for 3s or more, Absolute Penetration has a chance of triggering a Head Shot, killing the target outright (only works in PVP/GVG against non-MVP/Mini targets). Head Shot chance is (your LUK - target's LUK)*0.07%.

Thorough Through - Vital Part Strike
Lvl: 5 Passive
