Skills

Big Bang Siu Mai
Lvl: 5 LeadSkill CD: 20 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Range: 5

Channeled skill. Make a super-sized shumai by wrapping the stuffing made from an entire pig.
【Cooking Process】: The huge shumai skin spins in Liu Maoxing's hands, seemingly wrapping everything around. Enemies within 5 meters cannot attack, and cooking ends after 4 seconds.
【Cooking Succeeded】: The huge shumai wraps all nearby enemies, turning them into "Shumai People" who cannot attack or move for 3 seconds. The effect cannot be cleared.
Cooldown time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Coiled Dragon Pot
Lvl: 10 Passive

A piece of legendary cookware that can make sauce in an instance. Reduces variable and fixed cast times of all teammates 8m or less from Liu Maoxing by 30%, and boosts all debuff RES of Liu Maoxing during [Cooking Process] by 50%.

Comet Fried Rice
Lvl: 10 LeadSkill CD: 18 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Range: 8

Channeled skill. Fried rice made with spinach, bok choy, green onions, and rice.
【Cooking Process】: Liu Maoxing raises a rosary in his right hand and clasps his left hand in front of his chest, praying for the people of Jiuhua. Every 0.5 seconds, it provides a damage absorption shield to self and teammates within 8 meters, with an absorption value equal to self's magic attack *10, lasting for 5 seconds. The shield can stack and cannot be cleared (effect is quadrupled in PVP or GVG), and cooking ends after 1.5 seconds.
【Cooking Succeeded】: A comet falls, and the healthy and delicious aroma refreshes and energizes everyone. The shield duration for self and teammates within 8 meters extends to 60 seconds and can absorb life loss.

Crystal Phoenix
Lvl: 5 LeadSkill CD: 14 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 200 Range: 8

A channeling skill. Removes both ends of bean sprouts and arranges the sprouts into the shape of a phoenix.
[Cooking Process]: Bean sprouts simply fried emit a refreshing natural aroma that delights people nearby. Negates death for self and teammates within 8m. Cooking ends in 3s.
[Cooking Succeeded]: Leaves a plate of [Crystal Phoenix] at the current spot for teammates within 3m and self to consume. Can be consumed 3 times in total and up to once every 7s. The dish lasts 10s. Only one can exist at a time.

The crunch sound when chewing crispy bean sprouts is appetizing. Negates death for self for 5s. The effect cannot be cleared.
The cooldown is fixed.

Forever-Spirit Knife
Lvl: 10 Passive

Legendary kitchenware that can restore the freshness of ingredients. Increases the damage of Liu Maoxing's food by 30%. After 【Great Magic Panda Tofu】 deals damage, the lower attribute between water and fire attacks will temporarily increase to match the higher one.

Magical Panda Tofu
Lvl: 10 LeadSkill Magic CD: 1.2 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 500 Range: 5

Channeled skill. Cook black and white layered tofu using a lard board as a separator.
【Cooking Process】: Black and white tofu alternately falls into a carefully prepared soup, splashing the rich spicy flavor that makes everyone around crave for more. Every 0.5 seconds, it deals fire attribute magic damage equal to magic attack *1800% and water attribute magic damage of the same ratio to enemies within 5 meters, with a 50% chance to inflict 【Burn】. Cooking ends after 5 seconds.
【Cooking Succeeded】: A plate of 【Great Magic Panda Tofu】 is created at the current location. The numbing and spicy Sichuan cuisine makes people sweat profusely. Enemies entering the 5-meter range will consume it, up to 5 times, at most once per second, lasting for 5 seconds.

After consumption, they take fire attribute magic damage equal to Liu Maoxing's magic attack *2800%.

Mobile cooking
Lvl: 10 Passive

In order to deliver the freshest dishes to diners, Liu Maoxing learned during his stay at Midgards to move around during [Cooking Process], but his MOV SPD cannot exceed 100%%. For every 3 points of STR, Max MOV SPD increases by 1%%. For every 1 points of STR, Liu Maoxing's Max HP increases by 500.

Mountain Treasures Hotpot
Lvl: 10 LeadSkill CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 8

Channeled skill. A rich soup made with various mountain delicacies and lobster, combining the flavors of "mountain" and "sea".
【Cooking Process】: The mountain delicacies complement the lobster in the pot, and the resulting soup fills everyone with strength. Every 0.5 seconds, it increases Phy. Dmg Inc. and M.Dmg Inc. by 10% for self and teammates within 8 meters, stacking up to 5 times, lasting for 120 seconds. Cooking ends after 1.5 seconds.
【Cooking Succeeded】: A plate of 【Mountain Treasures Hotpot】 is created at the current location. The refreshing "mountain" and sweet "sea" flavors blend perfectly. Teammates and self within 3 meters can consume it. It can be consumed up to 5 times, at most once every 10 seconds, lasting for 10 seconds, with only one existing at a time.

After consumption, cooldown time and cast delay are reduced by 30% (when stacked with other cooldown reduction effects, the total reduction does not exceed 50%), lasting for 10 seconds.

Sea Bream Continental Seal
Lvl: 5 LeadSkill CD: 20 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec Range: 12

Channeled skill. Make four regional style dishes using four parts of a sea bream.
【Cooking Process】: The process of scraping fish scales with a special steel wire is stress-relieving, restoring 5% of maximum HP and MP per second for teammates and self within a 12-meter range. The recovery amount increases by 100% for each additional teammate in the range, up to a maximum increase of 400%. Cooking ends after 5 seconds.
【Cooking Succeeded】: The sea bream opens like a fan, forming a landscape painting that stirs emotions and pride. If there are 4 or more teammates (including self) around when cooking ends, all teammates and self within the range become invincible for 4 seconds (can be applied once every 10 seconds).

Super Chef
Lvl: 10 Passive

As a super chef, Liu Maoxing has a discerning palate. When he eats local dishes of Midgards, his Max HP increases by 5% and his M.ATK increases by 10%. Lasts 240m. Stacks up to 6 times.

Basic Dish
Lvl: 1 Attack Magic Range: 5

Liu Maoxing demonstrates his proficiency in cooking basics to impress spectators nearby. Prepares for 1s and deals Neutral M.DMG equal to M.ATK*300% to enemies within 5m.

Genius Cook
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Liu Maoxing's channeled skill is called 【Cooking Process】, which can be interrupted at any time. During the 【Cooking Process】, Liu Maoxing's damage taken is reduced by 10%. There is a 50% chance to trigger 【Cooking Succeeded】 at the end of the channeling time.

Lv4. For every 5 Luk, Liu Maoxing's 【Cooking Succeeded】 chance increases by 1%. When the 【Cooking Succeeded】 chance reaches 100%, each additional 5 Luk increases Skill Dmg Reduc. and Auto Attack Reduction by 1%.

Lv7. Liu Maoxing's 【Cooking Process】 will not be interrupted, and while cooking, Fei will also help cook the same food at the current location.
