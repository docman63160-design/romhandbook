Skills

Butterfly Ripple
Lvl: 10 ShadowRipple Magic CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 330 Range: 7 Cast Time: 5

Butterflies flutter their wings, creating ripples in the past. Sara Alin continuously generates circles of outwardly spreading butterfly Ripples in the designated area, with a period of 1.5 seconds, creating 3 ripple rings with different radii (2~3 meters, 3~4 meters, 4~5 meters). Enemies who touch the ripple rings will suffer dark attribute magic damage of magic attack *3600%, and at the same time, apply 【Dark Spell】. The skill effect lasts for 6 seconds.

Heavenly Justice
Lvl: 5 Passive

The old nobles believe that Str corresponds to responsibility, and Sara Alin's magical damage skills will additionally calculate the bonus and reduction of size. Increase self-inflicted 30% damage to L Size and 15% damage to M Size.

Inner Fire's Blessing
Lvl: 10 Passive

Sara Alin's mother protects her Ping'an with Wish Power, increasing her own 50% Final Dmg Reduc. in the 【Hiding】 state, and recovering 5% of her maximum health per second.

Manipulate Nature
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 6 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 320 Range: 7 Cast Time: 5

Apply telekinesis to the designated enemy, causing *3600% Ghost magical damage and applying 【Cast】. Every 3 points of Luk increases the damage by 1%, and if there are no other teammates within a 5-meter range of the enemy, an additional 100% damage is dealt.

Maple by Flame
Lvl: 10 Attack Magic CD: 1 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 240 Range: 7 Cast Time: 3

Sara Alin summons flames to attack the locked target direction, dyeing the maple leaves of Payon red. The Fire dragon deals magical fire damage to all enemies *3600% on the path and, after a 0.5-second delay, deals magical fire damage *3600% to all enemies within a 4-meter radius of the locked target. Both attacks inflict the 【Fire Spell】 on enemies.

Return to Sea
Lvl: 1 SaraIrenaUltimate Magic CD: 15 sec Skill Delay: 2 sec SP: 500 Range: 5

The ancient noble secret technique can summon fire, dark, and psychic Wish Power, attacking in a fan-shaped pattern in the designated direction. While in motion, it causes elemental magic damage to all enemies on the path, and upon reaching the area, it rises into the sky causing elemental damage to all enemies within a 4-meter range, and also triggering a cycle of Wish Power Ripple. All damage will be added with the corresponding Wish Spell. The Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced.

Sky-earth Resonance
Lvl: 10 Passive

Inflict different phantasm effects on the enemy according to the Wish Spell on them and the Wish Talisman you choose:
●[Fire＋Fire—>True Flame]: Take Fire M.DMG equal to M.ATK*3600% per sec for 5s
●[Fire＋Dark—>Residual Ash]: Take Dark M.DMG equal to M.ATK*3600% per sec for 5s
●[Fire＋Ghost—>Heartburn]: Take Ghost M.DMG equal to M.ATK*3600% per sec for 5s
●[Dark＋Dark—>Nothingness]: Attack range and casting range reduced to 4m for 6s
●[Dark＋Ghost—>Soul Penalty]: Debuff RES reduced by 30% for 10s
●[Ghost＋Ghost—>Unreal]: Auto attack banned for 6s
Different phantasm effects can stack.

Stealth Technique
Lvl: 10 Passive

Ancient noble secret technique, when in the 【Hiding】 state, deals an additional 50% damage to enemies not in the 【Hiding】 state; when not in the 【Hiding】 state, deals an additional 50% damage to enemies in the 【Hiding】 state.

Sudden Farewell
Lvl: 5 Buff CD: 24 sec Skill Delay: 1.5 sec SP: 300 Range: 7 Cast Time: 3 Fixed Cast Time: 1

Applies Sudden Farewell to an enemy, banning them from targeting and being targeted by their teammates for 12s, and inflicting [Manipulate Nature] on them every 2s. The effect can't be cleared. Its cooldown is fixed and can't be reduced.

Tarsal Bone Maggot
Lvl: 5 Passive

Sara Alin never misses her prey, her fire attribute damage has a 50% chance of putting the enemy into 【burning】, dark attribute damage has a 50% chance of putting the enemy into 【Dark】, Ghost damage has a 50% chance of putting the enemy into 【Silence】.

Wish Chant
Lvl: 5 SoulMark CD: 28 sec Skill Delay: 1 sec SP: 100 Range: 7 Cast Time: 3

Sara Alin adds the following special effects for herself and one teammate based on different talismans:
●【Fire Talisman】: Ignore the enemy's physical and magical defense
●【Dark Talisman】: Enter the 【Hiding】 state and attacks will not reveal the character
●【Ghost Talisman】: Immune to all abnormal statuses

During the 【Wish to praise】, debuffs inflicted by the character are no longer judged based on whether they cause damage, the special effect lasts for 10 seconds, the effect cannot be dispelled, and the Cooldown Time is fixed and cannot be reduced. Talisman effects cannot be stacked, and can only be added to a maximum of one teammate.
