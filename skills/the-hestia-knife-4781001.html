The Hestia Knife
Lvl: 1 Passive

Lv1. Bell's main hand is always equipped with The Hestia Knife, a weapon personally forged by Hephaistos, the God of the Forge, at Hestia's request, costing two hundred million valis. It boasts a high magic conductivity rate. The weapon itself possesses ability values and can undergo Growth based on the wielder's abilities, making it a living weapon. Its stats change according to the following values:
● Str: Every 10 points increase by *0.01%* lifesteal.
● Agi: Every point increases *0.01%* Auto Attack damage.
● VIT: Every 10 points restore *0.01%* of max HP per second.
● Int: Every point increases *0.01%* Ignore M.Def.
● Dex: Every point increases *0.01%* Phy. Dmg Inc. and Pen.
● Luk: Every point increases *0.01%* magic Critical chance.

When performing an Auto Attack, it deals additional magic damage equal to 100% M.Atk and can trigger magic Critical.

Lv4. You can select two attributes of The Hestia Knife to double their effects.

Lv7. During Auto Attack, The Hestia Knife executes smooth Successive attacks, eliminating the pre- and post-attack animation delays, fully matching the attack frequency with Attack Spd.

function CommonFun.calcBuff_9481(srcUser, targetUser, a, b, c, d, lv)
  if srcUser == nil or targetUser == nil then
    return 0
  end
  local Vit = srcUser:GetProperty("Vit")
  local MaxHp = srcUser:GetProperty("MaxHp")
  local skilllv = srcUser:GetLernedSkillLevel(4781_PHANTOM_DANCER:THE_HESTIA_KNIFE)
  local skilllv_2 = srcUser:GetLernedSkillLevel(4790_PHANTOM_DANCER:ADVENTURER)
  local ManualLevel = srcUser:GetManualLevel()
  local Manual = 1
  if 1 <= skilllv_2 then
    Manual = 1 + math.min(math.floor(ManualLevel / 10), 5) * 0.02 * skilllv_2
  end
  local A = math.floor(Vit / 10) * 1.0E-4 * skilllv * Manual * MaxHp
  if srcUser:HasBuffID(140812_VIT_:MAX_STACK_0) and 4 <= skilllv then
    A = A * 2
  end
  return A
end
