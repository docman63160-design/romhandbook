Meltdown
Lvl: 20 Buff SP: 30 Range: 1.5

Enter 110 seconds of Meltdown, when attacking monsters, Enemy unit’s Def and Atk will reduce by 300 and 300 respectively. Player has a 20% chance to destroy targets' weapon, a 14% chance to destroy their armor; player's Auto Attack damage will increase by 30%.
